The Three Stars

Luzon

Luzon has the capital city of the Philippines which is Manila City.

Luzon covers an area of approximately 109,965 square kilometers (42,458 square miles), making it the largest island in the Philippines. It is located in the northern part of the Philippines.

Visayas

Known for its beautiful landscapes, rich cultural heritage, delicious cuisine, and warm hospitality.The Visayas is located in the central part of the Philippines and is composed of several islands. The major islands include Panay, Negros, Cebu, Bohol, Leyte, and Samar.

Mindanao

The second-largest and southernmost island in the Philippines, is a culturally diverse region with a rich history and natural beauty. Characterized by rugged terrain, mountains, and active volcanoes. Mindanao is known for its religious diversity; Muslim, Christian, and indigenous communities.
